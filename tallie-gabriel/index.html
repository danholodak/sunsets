Tallie Gabriel

Tallie Gabriel

An artist of many facets, Tallie flexes her creative muscles writing poetry and music, creating video vignettes and trademark cocktails, and showing off her impeccable sense of style! (She uses "the gasp method" to guide her thrifting).

Tallie played 5 songs for us beneath a beautiful bright pink sky - many of them from their debut EP 'little death'. It's one of our favorite releases from 2020 and we can't help but keep coming back to it.
