Stella.

Stella.

Stella. (the period is part of the name) is dance-pop artist Francis Aud's softer, more sentimental side-project. It began with the 2019 single Back to Me, the first song he played in his Sunsets session.

Stella.'s beautiful guitar melodies blend perfectly with lyrics that seem to dance around them, creating wonderfully sweet and charming songs.
