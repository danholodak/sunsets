The Velvicks

The Velvicks are a Rock and Roll group based out of Brooklyn. They have a heavy, loud, uptempo sound that injects a frenzied energy into any space they perform in.

The Velvicks played 4 songs for us, an epic but brief set, on a Brooklyn rooftop that looked more like the deck of a pirate ship.
